Intern Responsibilities

At Balaji System Informatics Solutions Private Limited, Interns are expected to be professionally well-versed with basic ethics, which includes arriving on time (as specified by the company), notifying the team leader of any deviations from the established schedule, and dressing to the standards of the organization and the work being performed. Respect the organization's reporting structure and follow the policies and procedures of the organization.

During the internship tenure, intern will be working on following verticals (Remotely):

1. Coordinate with the whole team to improve the website and add requested functionalities.
2. Handle full-stack, front-end, back-end, user experience, Bootstrap, etc.
3. Test the website daily and maintain server error reports.
4. Accommodate front-end or server-side development.
5. Work on UI, cross-browser compatibility, general web functions, and standards.

Perks and Benefits

Experience Letter

This letter from the company confirms the work experience gained by the intern during the internship program. It is an essential document for visa processing and future employability.

Program Outcomes Report

This report summarises the knowledge, professional skills, and various personality attributes of an intern. The PO report helps in earning academic credits for your further studies.

Recommendation Letter

This document assesses and recommends the qualities, characteristics, and capabilities of an intern based on their performance observed during the internship period.

Snacks and Beverages

This internship includes free snacks and beverages, making your work experience more awesome. Remember free food doesn't necessarily come with easy responsibilities.

Flexible Work Hours

This company gives the intern a choice to opt for convenient work timings ensuring certain limits are maintained, like minimum and the maximum number of hours of work per day.

Pre Placement Offer

Depending on the performance of the intern, the company might extend a full-time job offer to deserving candidates. With an offer like this, you can always have an alternate placement option.

5 Days a Week

This internship comes with a five-day-work week. Interns are given a choice to opt for suitable work arrangements based on a five-day-work and two-day-off pattern.

Weekend Movie Tickets

The company offers free movie tickets to its interns. This is an added benefit to spice up your weekend plans by watching your favorite movie with your friends and colleagues.

Incentives

The company rewards those interns who perform exceptionally well. This internship not only gives hands-on experience but also an opportunity to add new achievements on your CV.

Paid Birthday Holiday

All interns are eligible to receive a day off with pay for their birthday this year. Interns need to submit their birthday day off request, in writing, or by an email, to their supervisor.

Pickup & Drop Service

The company offers a pickup and drop facility from the nearest landmarks and workplace. This facility primarily aims to make it convenient for interns to reach their destination in a hassle-free manner.

Indoor Games Facility

Indoor games boost up energy levels and creativity. The company allows its interns to play games at the workplace. These indoor games help you get timely breaks and keep you stay focused.

Free Accommodation

The employer offers rent-free housing to interns. The perk is highly valuable in the case of outstation internships because it saves the intern's time and money that goes into finding accommodation.

Internship Certificate

Interns receive this certificate after completion of their internship. It certifies a student's experience, skills, and performance in a particular area of work with the company.

Phone Bill Reimbursement

Reach out to whoever you want to without worrying about the phone bill as the company offers phone bill reimbursement for the interns. The phone bill also covers internet packs.

Mentor Programs

Mentor programs are aimed to give you guidance and training under industry experts. Here you get a chance to meet, talk, and learn from top leadership and higher management of the company.

Team-Building Activities

The company organizes fun, team-building activities that help in breaking the ice and building new connections. These outdoor and indoor activities take place on a quarterly or half-yearly basis.

Gym Facilities

Staying healthy with a desk job requires effort and a healthy state of mind. Free gym facilities help interns to stay fit without worrying about paid memberships.

Promotions

The company offers a promotion to those interns who show exceptional performance. Getting promoted leverages the current position of the intern for new opportunities.

Medical Benefits

The internship comes with medical benefits that take care of your medical expenses in case you fall ill. It also takes care of medical costs incurred in case of an accident or injury.

Career Pathing & Training

Unsure which career path should you take during or post internship? Senior Consultants at the company will guide you choose the best career path based on your skill sets and interest level.

Corporate Discounts

From restaurants to the gym, and favorite books, get company discounts at a lot of places. This internship offers a premium experience and organizational credibility benefits to its interns.

Open Offices

Sit and work from anywhere in the office. Need help? Just reach out to your manager, probably working from the bean bag next to you. No cubicles, no boundaries. Only open offices.

Stock Ownership Plans

The company offers stock ownership along with stipend and other perks. This rare perk envisages growing the value of the intern as the company grows in the future.

Additional Instructions To Applicants

1. Selected candidates for Research at Microsoft will require to bring supporting documentation which includes all the marksheets, updated resume, certificates, recommendations letter (if any), etc.

2. Candidates currently pursuing their graduation or recent graduates can apply to this internship.
